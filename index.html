Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about WeAreG's services, from security to implementation.

What is WeAreG?

WeAreG is a company specialized in AI infrastructure for businesses. We help companies automate processes, organize data and structure professional databases using artificial intelligence. Everything tailored to your operation.

How does process automation work?

We analyze your company's manual and repetitive processes and build automated AI workflows. From sending emails to processing data, everything runs automatically with high speed and reliability.

Is my data kept secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption and a secure architecture. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with regular security audits. Protecting your data is our top priority.

How does the spreadsheet migration work?

We gather all your scattered spreadsheets and data, then organize and structure them in a professional database. You end up with clean, accessible and easy-to-manage data, without losing any information.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. WeAreG handles all the technical side. You just tell us what your company's problems and processes are, and we implement the complete AI solutions for you.

How long does implementation take?

It depends on the complexity of the project. Simple automations can be ready in a few days, while more complete AI infrastructure projects take a few weeks. We always run a discovery beforehand to align timelines.

Do you build custom payment approval portals?

Yes. CAP (Centralized Approval Portal) is one of our flagship builds. We design centralized portals where every wire, ACH and disbursement goes through multistep approval flows, with supporting documents attached and a full audit trail of who approved what and when. Each portal is tailored to your team structure, approval limits and compliance requirements.

Can the approval portal integrate with our bank and ERP?

Yes. The portal connects to your existing banking partners, ERP and accounting tools so approvals flow into wire instructions, GL postings and notifications without manual rework. We map your real process during discovery and only automate what your team needs.

What's the investment?

We offer flexible plans based on your company's needs. Each project is quoted individually after discovery. No hidden fees and no long-term contracts. Get in touch for a custom quote.

How do I get in touch?

Reach out via our contact form, email or social media. Our team responds quickly and is ready to understand your company's needs.
